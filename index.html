About The Book

What does it mean to be XYZ?


Nancy Lublin, CEO of DoSomething.org, was asked to write a book about how DoSomething.org has created such a great culture.

Her response? “Not alone. I’d be a douche if I did this without my team." And, that is where this book began.

The XYZ Factor is a new kind of organizational culture where innovation, accessibility, and transparency are the norm. An XYZ organization's employees are challenged, engaged and excited to produce.

Any company can become an XYZ company. This book is your blueprint.

This guidebook is your key to answering the questions your company has looming over its cubicles: How do I motivate my staff? How do I form the right partnerships? Where’s the right place to start with data collection? … just to name a few. The answers to these questions and more are sprawled across nine chapters written by various staff members of the DoSomething.org team.

When you open The XYZ Factor, you’re getting the recipe for the awesome-sauce that has driven the success of one of the world’s largest organizations for young people and social change.
